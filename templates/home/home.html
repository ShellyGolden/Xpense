What is Xpense?

The Xpense projects goal is to be an open source platform for personal financial management and improvement by monitoring, sharing and discovering other peoples financial management plans.

By comparing your situation of life and financial management you can read tips and learn how you can be a better financial manager of your own home and family

How to start?

So firstly you can take a look at the global page where you can see all the public houses that are public and see their average expenses.

you can also signup and fill your own data and keep up with your expenses.

What's in it for you?

You can keep your expenses data and see the data presented with graphs by year and divided monthly, see global houses financial management filtered by your liking.

in the tips section you can see tips posted on popular categories and post some of your own.
